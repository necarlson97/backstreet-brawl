Contact Rules
Rule
Most cards require some kind of contact. smack/smash require that the limb has moved during this turn to get to it's destination:
smack ‑ moved at least a forearm‑length smash ‑ moved at least a limb‑length a smack/smash can only be 'claimed' on one card, i.e, you can't simultaneously Knuckle Strike and Bloody Nose.
