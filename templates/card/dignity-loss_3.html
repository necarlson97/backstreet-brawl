Dignity Loss
That bastard. They'll pay. Here we go, a clean opening - your nails flash, and bicep flexes - this is going to be devastating. A sneering smile begins to spread across your lips - but is halted. Somewhere inside you, a child tugs at your sleeve. A stunned grunt leaps from your throat. Of course, the youth has your face, not bruised from combat, uncontorted by time. They don't say anything. But their whole, tiny form pleads. You can see their eyes, as if level with your own. Scleras unimaginably white. Pupils unbearably dark. Together, you cry.
