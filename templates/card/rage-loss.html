Rage Loss
Yes. Yes! Each blow you deliver -and receive- brings sharp, delectable notes of pain. The skin-seams at your knuckles and elbow fray, and begin to pull apart. Red velvet curtains opening for the show. You punch now not to subdue them, not to win - but to feel the bone-rattling ache that travels like storm surge through your metacarpal stones, like vibrato strings up the bow of your forearm, like pneumatic hammering through the humerus, to the collarbone, to the gooseflesh at the nape of your neck. The blinding wrath transmogrifies within you, a changeling hate - you don't hate them, you've forgotten about them. But you love to hate yourself. Masochism molts within you. When you are done, your smile will be unrecognizable.
