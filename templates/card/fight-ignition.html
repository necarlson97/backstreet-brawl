Fight Ignition
Rule
Lay out these Rule reminder cards on the table To start playing, each player should: * Draw 9 Action Cards * Lay, face down, one of each Loss Card (8 total) * Place a d6 on each Loss Card, all starting at 6 * Place your figure in the stand, upright, with hands/arms however you like. Whichever player has the biggest bruise goes first. If neither is bruised, whomever bled last.
