Keep Breathing
Rule
At the start of your turn, gain focus and stamina from your stance:
standing: feet flat on ground, hips aligned between the feet, hips above knee‑level
+2 stamina +2 focus
crouching: feet on ground, hips aligned between the feet, hips above knee‑level
+1 stamina +2 focus
sitting: hips below knee‑level, chest above knee‑level
+2 stamina +1 focus
prone: hips and chest below knee‑level
+3 stamina
off balance: at least one limb on the ground
+2 stamina ‑1 focus
airborne: nothing on the ground
+2 stamina
Whenever a pose is could be multiple, the card‑player can choose which.
