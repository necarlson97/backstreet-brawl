Pain Loss
Okay, hold on. Blink. Cough. Unclench your jaw. You got this. But then another blow knocks stars into your eyes, and spins you round, palms landing on the gritty earth. The warrior within wills you to turn, to defend yourself, to guard your face, to rear back for another - No. No more. Barley have the words echoed from ear to ear before you are crawling -no, pawing -no, scrambling to get free. To get away from them, but to get farther away from yourself. From the mangled body you choose, when you choose violence.
